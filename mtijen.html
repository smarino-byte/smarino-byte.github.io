Mount Ijen

East Java, Indonesia

Elevation 2,769 m
Province East Java
Vulcanic arc Lesser Sunda Islands
Last eruption 1999

This vocano was more of a challenge that Mount Bromo, but still as beautiful. Same as Mt. Bromo, it involved a wake up call at 2am, tea or hot chocolate and, of course, fried banana. There was a big difference though, it actually incolved a hike. BUT, the really peculiar thing about this volcano, is that, if the timing is correct, you can acually see the pheonoenon of blue fire!

Now, what is blue fire? It's actually quite simple, it's just a chemical reaction caused by the ignition of sulfuric gases. These gases escape from volcanic vents, and when they come into contact with air, they burn with a vivid blue color. However, if your guide tells you that there is a very slight chance that you will experience this pheonomenon, please listen to him. Me and a friend got separated by the rest of the group because we were walking a little bit slower, and theywere sprinting up, almost running, that fro me and Lucca, was just not in the plan.

I have some advice for anybody who is planning on doing this hike:

  1. Do not eat too much fried banana before starting to go up, it will hurt your stomach
  2. Wear many many layers, it will get very cold at the top
  3. Do not sprint up the volcano if your guide tells you that there is a very little chance that you will see the blue fire
  4. Skip going down to the volcanic vents and enjoy the beautiful sunrise overlooking the sea
