Mount Rinjani

Lombok, Indonesia

Elevation 3,726 m
Province West Nusa Tenggara
Vulcanic arc Lesser Sunda Islands
Last eruption 2015

Now this, this was really a challenge. I don't really know what I was thinking when I decided to do this hike, since I had never done any kind of hiking prior to this. This was truly very hard for me and I honesty don't know if, looking back, I would do again.

So, the important part to not of this hike is how much elevation you do in the arc of 2 days. Here is a look at the schedule:

  • Day 1: The day actually starts at night, where the company you booked the hike with brings you to their hostel to sleep for the night. They also give you a prwview of what to expect. And then to bed before the big day
  • Day 2
    • Wake up call: 6am
    • Sart treck: 9am - starting point of elevation 1156m
    • Treck to Pos 1: 1300m
    • Treck to Pos 2: 1500m
    • Treck to Pos 3: 1800m
    • Treck to the Crater Rim (where you rest for the night): 2639m
  • Day 3
    • Wake up call: 1 am
    • Treck to the summit to see the sunrise (if you get there in time to see it): 3726m
    • Back to camp and a well deserved breakfast
    • Treck back to the sarting point

As you ca see, you will almost 3000 meters of elevation in under 24 hours, for then to walk them all back down in just under 6 hours. Overall, it was a lot for the body to endure on a radom Thursday with no preparation.
